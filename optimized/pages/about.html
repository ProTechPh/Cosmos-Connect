About Cosmos Connect

Making NASA's vast collection of space data accessible to everyone through a clean, user-friendly web interface.

Our Mission

To democratize access to NASA's incredible space data and inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts, researchers, and explorers.

Cosmos Connect serves as a bridge between NASA's powerful APIs and the curious minds eager to explore the cosmos. Our platform transforms complex scientific data into engaging, accessible experiences that anyone can understand and enjoy.

What We Offer

17+ NASA APIs

Access to NASA's most popular APIs including APOD, Mars Weather, Near-Earth Objects, Space Weather, and more.

Responsive Design

Fully responsive interface that works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Fast Performance

Optimized caching, lazy loading, and performance enhancements for the best user experience.

Accessibility

Built with accessibility in mind, ensuring everyone can explore the cosmos regardless of abilities.
