Understanding Space Weather

Solar Flares

Intense bursts of radiation from the Sun's surface that can disrupt radio communications and satellite operations.

Geomagnetic Storms

Disturbances in Earth's magnetosphere caused by solar wind, affecting GPS systems and power grids.

Coronal Mass Ejections

Large expulsions of plasma and magnetic field from the Sun's corona that can cause major space weather events.

Impact on Technology

Space weather can affect satellites, astronauts, airline operations, power grids, and GPS navigation systems.
